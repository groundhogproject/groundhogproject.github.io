The Groundhog Project

Collaboration: A place for sketches and ideas, no genre in particular, the idea is to share ideas, collaborate, and develop those that resonate.

Gathering: The goal for now is to gather ideas, to arrange, and to craft them. At this phase of the project we are looking to develop the ideas into songs rather than to produce them into their final state.

Keep it fresh: Whilst crafting we should try to avoid over listening to a sketch unless we are actively working to improve it.

Live performance is not a goal: we might consider a one-off performance in the future but it is not the focus of the project.

Emerging from hibernation: When we have enough ideas that coalesce, we will turn our focus to producting the ideas we have finished crafting and release an album.
